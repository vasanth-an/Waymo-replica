About us

leading the way towards on the
autonomous future

autonomous technology is at the heart of solutions for mobility, intralogistics and more. Our technology brings autonomous vehicles to life around the world.

Autonomous vehicles ready today

EasyMile provides software and complete solutions for driverless goods and passenger transport. It partners with blue-chip manufacturers to autonomize their vehicles.

The company’s global clients list includes major public transport operators, industrial sites and airports, and ports.

They usethe EZ10 public transport solution, the most deployed autonomous shuttle in the world, the TractEasy is a tow-truck solution deployed at a growing number of large vehicle manufacturing plants in both Europe and North America, as well as at major international airports, or the port terminal tractor, EZTug, which automates horizontal container transport.

EasyMile has deployed vehicles in over 400 locations around the world and is accelerating its commercialization.

Blue-chip suppliers, partners and investors
